How To Prevent Flooding From A Hurricane



Problem

Hurricanes have always been causing major floods and destroying people’s homes in the past. Many times people aren’t prepared but even if they are, floods have always managed to enter houses and flood people’s homes. How can we prevent the destruction of homes and protect people’s property in an easy and cost-effective way?

Existing Solutions

Many solutions have been made concerning this problem. In order to prevent water from entering people’s homes, people put sandbags by all their doorways. They also put tarp or duct tape around the doorway to seal it. There is even a floodgate, which is a large, water-tight door, made for homes but they are very expensive.

Existing Solution Pros Cons
Putting sandbags in front of a door. It's very cheap and easy. It doesn't keep water out that well. Water is still able to seep in.
Use tarp to cover the doorway. It's very cheap and easy. It doesn't seal the water out well enough. The water is still able to get in.
Cover the doorway with duct tape. It's very cheap and easy. It doesn't seal the water out entirely and the water can still find a way in.
Install a special house-floodate. It keeps the water out of the house very well and water almost never manages to get in. It's extremely expensive and unaffordable for many people, and impracticle.

Our Solution

The existing solutions are great. However, we want less costly and more effective ways of preventing floods. When we were researching, we found out that to prevent water from entering a tunnel, they use a floodgate- a big,custom-made, marine door, with an inflatable gasket surrounding it. Using the logic behind it, we thought of using an inflatable gasket to surround the door of a regular home. If it works for the tunnels, it can work for regular buildings.
